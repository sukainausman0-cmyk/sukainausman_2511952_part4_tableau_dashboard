# Dashboard Story

## Executive Summary

The dashboard shows a retail business with growing sales, profitable technology performance, clear regional differences, and risk from discounting, delays, and returns. Total sales are 738638.85, total profit is 59429.85, profit margin is 8.1%, and return rate is 19.7%.

## What Is Performing Well

Sales trend upward across the year, and West is the strongest region. Technology and high-value customer segments provide attractive profit opportunities.

## What Is Underperforming

Some category/sub-category combinations show weaker profit after discounting. Standard Class shipping creates longer delivery buckets, which may damage customer experience.

## Risks Visible

Deep discounts can hide weak profitability. Returns create margin leakage, especially when combined with low-profit categories. Delayed delivery can increase dissatisfaction and return risk.

## Opportunities Visible

Leadership can prioritize profitable categories, copy practices from the best region, and use filters to identify category-region combinations where sales are high but profit is weak.

## Recommended Actions

Protect inventory for high-profit categories, review discount approvals, monitor delayed shipping lanes, and investigate returned orders by region, segment, and category.

## Limitations and Next Analysis

The dashboard shows descriptive patterns, not causation. Next analysis should add marketing cost, customer lifetime value, product margin, competitor data, and cohort retention.
